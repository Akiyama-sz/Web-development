CULTURAL SIGNIFICANCE

Lusail Stadium, also known as Lusail National Stadium is a stadium with 80,000 seats in Lusail. It will host the final of the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

The Stadium is designed by Foster + Partners in a fittingly futuristic way. The sleek, bold shape of this arena and its sloping roof will stun fans at the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™. Yet that very shape is based upon bowls, vessels and art pieces used across the Middle East for centuries. The exterior is covered by elegant, muted gold, and with the passing of time, it will inspire an image of aged metal handicrafts. Adorned with intricate detail reminiscent of decorative wares from days gone by, the stadium bowl will feature a graceful, undulating trim.

Worth noting, the stadium's cable-membrane roof has similarly intricate pattern, with sections of membrane creating diamond-shaped element. At night, an advanced lighting system will maximize the interaction of the light and openings in the façade, mimicking the welcoming glow of a fanar lantern for approaching fans.

SUSTAINABILITY

Lusail stadium is not only aimed for hosting grand matches, but also will befits the city in following days. Forward planning and care for the environment are integral parts of this arena.

Sustainability work started by the moment when Lusail Stadium was envisaged and its location was chosen. Its position means fans can travel to matches via upgraded roads, such as the Doha Metro, shuttle buses – all suitably green methods of transport.

Materials are carefully sourced to align with considering both their manufacturing methods and future performance. Recycled water will be used to irrigate plants around the stadium, which will all be local varieties requiring little water. The efficient water fixtures and leak detection systems throughout the stadium complex conserves 40% more fresh water than in conventional stadiums.

All these measures help Lusail Stadium become an arena to usher in a new age of environmental responsibility.

TRANSPORTATION

By Metro

Option 1

  • Take the red line on metro and stop at Lusail Station
  • Walk for 5 minutes from the Lusail Station to Lusail Stadium entrance

Option 2

  • Take the red line on metro and stop at Legtaifiya Station
  • Take the orange line on the free Lusail Tram Service and stop at 2022 tram stop
  • Walk for 3 minutes from the 2022 tram stop to Lusail Stadium entrance
